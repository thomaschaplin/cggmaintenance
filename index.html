Welcome to CGG Maintenance

Cannon Garden Grounds Maintenance covers all aspects of garden maintenance including hedge trimming, small tree pruning, grass cutting and garden clearance.

CGG Maintenance are fully qualified and experience in garden maintenance. They are skilled, enthusiastic and offer competitive prices.
